My Mission

What will I do as your Regional Officer?

   My goal as a Regional Officer would be to improve communication both within and between chapters. In order to do this, I would like to implement various ideas to allow more information to be shared between members so that they may have the best TSA experience possible.

   For example, for newer members, selecting competitions may actually be a disappointing process. I myself recall looking at the long event list and feeling dismayed at the fact that I did not have the skills or knowledge to participate in many of the competitions offered. In order to address this, as a regional officer, I would implement a form system wherein skilled upperclassmen and newer members of TSA could submit their contacts in order to be connected, allowing the more experienced member to teach the less experienced.

   I would also like to improve communication between competition administrators and competitors. It seems that at almost every conference, an event is delayed, a schedule is changed, or something along those lines. I would like to propose a requirement for all delays or changes to be posted to a widely visible notice board at the competition. I would also like to advocate for more communication between competition administrators and host officers in order to increase the likelihood of the news getting out.

   Overall, my goal is simply to do my part in helping OKTSA be the best it can be.
